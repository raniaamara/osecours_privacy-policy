Osecours - Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application “Osecours” developed by Rania Amara. Information Usage Osecours is an emergency application designed to help users quickly contact emergency services and trusted contacts. The app may use the following features: * Location (GPS): to send your position to your emergency contacts during an SOS alert * SMS: to send emergency messages (SOS) to your selected contacts * Phone: to call emergency services or your contacts All data is used only for emergency purposes. Data Collection Osecours does NOT collect, store, or share your personal data on external servers. Your information stays on your device and is only used when you trigger an SOS. Data Sharing Your data (such as location) is only shared with your emergency contacts when you activate an SOS alert. The app does not sell or share data with third parties. First Aid Disclaimer The app provides first aid instructions for help during emergencies. These instructions do not replace professional medical assistance. Permissions The app may request access to: * Location * SMS * Phone These permissions are only used for core emergency features. Children’s Privacy The app is not intended for children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Changes This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future.
